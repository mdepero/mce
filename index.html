The Miami Notebook Program:

Proven to enhance the student computing experience.

The Program that combines the laptop computer you want with the service and support you need.

In the past seven years, over 15,000 students have become members of the Miami Notebook Program with outstanding results.

What are the advantages of the Miami Notebook Program?

  • The very best laptops configured to meet your academic and lifestyle needs for up to four years.
  • On-campus support and service provided by certified technicians, employed by Miami University's IT Services.
  • Access to loaner laptops if yours needs a repair.
  • A software image pre-installed including anti-virus/anti-spyware software, uPrint, Cisco AnyConnect, LibreOffice and more.
  • Get an academic discount, plus the extras you cannot get anywhere else.
  • The confidence of purchasing your laptop from the university owned bookstore.
